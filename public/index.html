📚 What is NLP? — Concepts used above

Tokenization

Splitting text into individual words or tokens.

Sentiment Analysis

Detecting if text is positive, negative, or neutral.

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Identifying real-world entities like people, places, and organizations.

Part-of-Speech Tagging (POS)

Labeling each word as noun, verb, adjective, etc.

TF-IDF (Keyword Extraction)

Finding the most important/unique words in the text.

Stemming

Reducing words to their base/root form (e.g. "running" → "run").
