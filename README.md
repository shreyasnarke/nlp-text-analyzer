🧠 I built a mini NLP Text Analyzer from scratch — and it taught me more than any tutorial ever did.

As a developer diving into AI & Machine Learning, I kept hearing the term Natural Language Processing (NLP) everywhere.

So instead of just reading about it — I built it.

Here's what the app does in real-time 👇

✅ Tokenization — splits your text into individual words
✅ Sentiment Analysis — detects if text is Positive 😊, Negative 😞, or Neutral 😐
✅ Named Entity Recognition (NER) — finds People, Places & Organizations
✅ POS Tagging — labels every word as Noun, Verb, or Adjective
✅ TF-IDF Keyword Extraction — finds the most important words
✅ Word Frequency — visual bar chart of top words
✅ Stemming — reduces words to their root form (running → run)
